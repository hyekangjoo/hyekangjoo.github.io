Kevin Hyekang Joo

I am a graduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I work as a research assistant on projects primarily concerning computer vision and machine learning with Advisor David W. Jacobs. I am particularly interested in Spatiotemporal Feature Analysis, Video Understanding, Motion Understanding, Generative Model, Image Construction & Manipulation, and Human Cognition Modeling.


Previously, I conducted research on Data Science and Data Visualization with Dr. Leilani Battle at UWash Interactive Data Lab (formerly UMD Battle Data Lab) and on Detection and Tracking with Dr. Mohammad Nayeem Teli at the University of Maryland. In addition, I have conducted research on Temporal Action Proposal Generation and 3D Reconstruction as an affiliate research assistant under Dr. Ngan Le at the AICV Lab based in the University of Arkansas.

I received a B.S. from the University of Maryland, College Park, in May 2021, where I majored in Computer Science with specialization in Machine Learning and minored in Technology Entrepreneurship.

I am still in process of deciding on my summer research internship. I am also available for a chat through email if you would like to discuss anything with me, including research collaboration.
